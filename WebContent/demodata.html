Real-Time Traffic Speed Data

The Streaming Analytics Traffic starter application uses data that comes from a public web site created by the New York City Department of Transportation (DOT).

The TMC maintains a map of traffic speed detectors throughout the City. The speed detector themselves belong to various city and state agencies.

Information about this real time feed can be found at: NYC DOT Traffic Speed

The specific traffic data used in this demo is from: Demo Data
